From the kitchen

Cooking as
discipline.

"Good cooking is mostly about not getting in the way of good ingredients."
A principle I apply to transformation projects too.
I grew up in Sicily, where food is not a hobby — it's infrastructure. Cooking seriously started when I moved to Rome for university and had to rebuild what I'd taken for granted at home.

My kitchen is seasonal and regional. I'm interested in technique, in the logic behind recipes, in what changes when you understand why something works rather than just following steps. Pasta, slow braises, vegetables treated with care. No shortcuts.
